# Startup & Tech Quick Facts

## Valuations & Fundraising
- Pre-money vs post-money: Post-money = pre-money + investment amount
- A "unicorn" is a private company valued at $1B+
- SAFE = Simple Agreement for Future Equity (not a loan, not equity yet)
- Dilution: each funding round dilutes existing shareholders proportionally
- Rule of 40: growth rate + profit margin should exceed 40% for healthy SaaS

## Common Misquotes
- "Move fast and break things" was retired by Facebook/Meta in 2014
- Moore's Law is about transistor density, not raw performance
- "The cloud" is just someone else's computer (oversimplification but directionally true)
- First-mover advantage is largely a myth — Google wasn't the first search engine

## AI / LLM Facts
- GPT-4 parameter count was never officially confirmed by OpenAI
- "AGI" has no universally agreed-upon definition
- Transformer architecture was introduced in "Attention Is All You Need" (2017, Google)
- Training cost ≠ inference cost (training is one-time, inference is ongoing)

## Business Metrics
- ARR = Annual Recurring Revenue
- MRR = Monthly Recurring Revenue (ARR / 12)
- CAC = Customer Acquisition Cost
- LTV = Lifetime Value (should be > 3x CAC for healthy business)
- Burn rate = monthly cash spend minus revenue
- Runway = cash in bank / monthly burn rate
